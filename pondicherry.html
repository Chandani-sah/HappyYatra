PONDICHERRY



PARADISE BEACH



Also known as Plage Paradiso, this is one of the best beaches in Pondicherry that boasts of crystal clear waters and pristine sands. Tourists here can indulge in their favourite snacks at a number of beach shacks and beverages along with enjoying the numerous beach activities at Paradise Beach. A beautiful ferry ride takes you to the beach and worth every penny for its aesthetic surroundings. Paradise Beach is among those spectacular Pondicherry Beaches where tourists can look forward to an unforgettable sunrise along with a panoramic view of the ocean. Location: Paradise Beach can be found 2 kilometres away from Chunnambar Boat House Activities: Swimming, beach volleyball, fishing etc. Distance from Pondicherry Bus Stand: It is located at a distance of 8 kilometres from the bus stand



AROUVILLE ASHRAM



Conceived as the 'Universal Town', the Auroville Ashram is an experimental township - the idea is to create a city where people from across the world of all cultures and traditions come and live together in peace.

Founded in 1926 by Sri Aurobindo and a disciple of Shri Aurobindo, French Lady known as 'Maa' or 'The Mother', the Aurobindo Ashram is a place where you can relax and calm your inner being. The project was supported by the Govt. of India, and the UNESCO passed a resolution in 1966 commending this as "a project of importance to the future of humanity". The town was officially inaugurated in 1968.
